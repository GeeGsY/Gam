Open World - ANANTA

Described as an "urban open-world RPG" and originally titled, Project Mugen, Ananta can be summed up as a mix of Genshin Impact, Persona, Spider-Man, and Grand Theft Auto. If that sounds like an eclectic mix of big-budget titles, you'd be right. Not only is NetEase looking to build an immersive open-world game, there is a lot more going on under the hood that sets Ananta apart from its various influences. If you can't wait to hit those mean streets, we've got all the latest news surrounding the Ananta release date.



What is Ananta?

Ananta is an upcoming free-to-play urban open-world RPG developed by Naked Rain, a subsidiary of NetEase Games. Set in the sprawling metropolis of Neo-Tokyo, players will assume the role of a customizable protagonist navigating a city teeming with life, intrigue, and danger. The game promises a rich narrative experience, blending elements of cyberpunk aesthetics with traditional RPG mechanics.

Ananta Release Date

We expect the Ananta release date to arrive sometime in 2026. Although an official launch window hasn't been confirmed yet, it's likely that developer Naked Rain will host several beta tests ahead of the full release..
