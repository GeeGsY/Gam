Wuthering Waves

Free-to-play open world action RPG

9.5
By Leonard on Jun 5, 2024

Wuthering Waves is a free-to-play open world action RPG from Kuro Games. It blends sweeping exploration, action-based combat, and gacha-style progression into a live-service experience with strong worldbuilding.

Gameplay

Combat is fast but sometimes lacks clarity when many effects are on-screen. Still, there are great moments where the game's systems click beautifully.

The Good

  • Interesting design ideas
  • Energetic combat

The Bad

  • Pacing can be uneven

Conclusion

Wuthering Waves' flashy combat and enticing exploration are a lot of fun, even if its story is a bland, exposition-heavy slog.

Game Details

  • Developer: Kuro Games
  • Publisher: Kuro Games
  • Release Date: May 23, 2024
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, Android, iOS, GeForce Now, Microsoft Windows, Mac operating systems
