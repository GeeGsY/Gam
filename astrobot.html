Astrobot

Charming platforming with tight controls.

9.0
By Leonard on Nov 10, 2024

Astrobot is an affectionate, tightly designed platformer with inventive level design and playful presentation. It nails the feel of classic platforming while adding modern touches.

Platforming and Design

Levels are varied, each with its own mechanics and set pieces. Movement is responsive and every gadget feels purposeful.

The Good

  • Polished controls and pacing
  • Beautiful level presentation

The Bad

  • Short runtime for completionists

Conclusion

Astrobot is a delightful platformer that showcases charm and careful design. Highly recommended for fans of the genre.

Game Details

  • Developer: Team ASOBI
  • Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
  • Release Date: September 6, 2024
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5
  • Genre: action-adventure

The Good

  • Charming presentation
  • Excellent controls

The Bad

  • Relatively short
