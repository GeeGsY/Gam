Elden Ring

A vast, rewarding action-RPG.

9.7
By Leonard on Feb 25, 2022

Elden Ring expands FromSoftware's formula into an open world without losing the focused challenge that makes their games special. It's a masterclass in design and atmosphere.

World and Combat

The combination of freedom, brutal combat, and thoughtful encounters creates moments of discovery that you'll remember long after finishing the game.

The Good

  • Expansive, cohesive open world
  • Rewarding combat and progression

The Bad

  • Occasional performance issues on older hardware

Conclusion

Elden Ring is a landmark action-RPG that blends exploration and challenge into a rich, unforgettable experience.

Game Details

  • Developer: FromSoftware Inc.
  • Publisher: Bandai Namco
  • Release Date:February 25, 2022
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Microsoft Windows, Xbox Series X and Series S, Xbox One
  • Genre: Action role-playing game, Adventure game, Fighting game, Platform game

The Good

  • Massive world with focused encounters
  • Deep customization and build options

The Bad

  • Some accessibility rough edges for newcomers
