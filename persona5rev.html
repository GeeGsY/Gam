Persona 5: The Phantom X

2025 role-playing adventure

9.5
By Leonard on Oct 1, 2025

Persona 5: The Phantom X is a 2025 RPG developed by Black Wings Game Studio and published by Perfect World. It updates the series with fresh systems and a global-scale story while keeping the stylish aesthetics and social-sim depth fans love.

Systems and Story

The game balances daily life, relationships, and Persona battles in a way that makes time management feel rewarding rather than a chore.

The Good

  • Memorable characters and stylish presentation
  • Deep combat and social systems

The Bad

  • Length can be intimidating

Conclusion

Persona 5 is a landmark JRPG that combines style, systems, and story in a package that's easy to recommend.

Game Details

  • Developer: Atlus, Arc Games Inc., Sega
  • Publisher: Arc Games Inc., Sega
  • Release Date:June 26, 2025
  • Platforms: Android, iOS, Microsoft Windows
  • Genre: Role-playing video game, Free To Play, Adventure

The Good

  • Stylish presentation and fantastic music
  • Deep, interlocking systems

The Bad

  • Very long commitment
